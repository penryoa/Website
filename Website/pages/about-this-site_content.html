about this site

intentions

I first started making this site at the beginning of June 2020 with some simple intentions:

  • grow my skills by trying out new tools (like Bootstrap, Angular, etc)
  • show off some of those skills
  • create a platform that feels like "me"

There's a ton of things I look forward to doing and I'm only starting out. Having my own website lets me document my journey ~in style~.

a little anecdote

For my senior project (August 2019 - May 2020), I spent most of my time working with PHP, HTML, and CSS. I was most proud of making two \"buttons\" using only HTML/CSS that would toggle visibility of a section, one above and one below the section to toggle.

So it looked like this:


|------------------|
|--button 1 --|
|------------------|
Some content in a div
|------------------|
|--button 2 --|
|------------------|


Button 1 and 2 aren't buttons, but tons of fun because they're just HTML checkbox inputs styled as buttons. I used classes and made it so if the content was hidden, Button 2 was also hidden.
In retrospect, this isn't super impressive. But at the time, I had no experience and this felt like a huge success. :)
